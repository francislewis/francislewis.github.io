Videography

I’m a freelance videographer, filmmaker and cameraman with several years of experience in the industry. I work all over the UK but I spend most of my time in Bristol, London and Swansea. I regularly work directly with clients, taking their ideas through the conceptual and pre-production stages all the way through to shooting, post production and delivery. Tailoring services to each client’s goals and budget I can provide every aspect of production from story-boarding, filming and editing, to crewing and project management. I’ve worked on a range of projects from music videos to christenings to conferences to TV adverts. You can have a look at some of my past work below and on my YouTube. I’m always on the lookout for new projects, if you or your company has an idea then get in touch!

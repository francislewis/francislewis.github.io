G2K

Alongside an amazing team of people I run events under the brand ‘Get to Know’ (G2K) in South Wales and the South West. After being disappointed with a lot of the local club nights at the time, we started throwing outdoor forest and beach parties that quickly grew close to thousands in capacity. After 2 years (and a few sell outs) we’ve grown a lot and expanded into new venues including nightclubs. What sets us apart is our production value and artistic direction, helping us to achieve (what we believe are) immersive experiences you rarely get anywhere else. We host a range of genres within electronic and underground music with the aim of focusing back on the dance, not the DJ, the judgement or the phones. If this interests you, check out our Facebook and Instagram pages, if you’d like to get involved in any capacity (DJs/Takeovers/Bookings/Collaborating etc.) then just shoot us a message.

THE ECLECTIC SHOEBOX

The Eclectic Shoebox is a weekly show I have on BURST Radio with my good mate Rohan. We save you the hassle of keeping on top of new releases within music, film and media by picking the best from each week across an unlimited range of genres. We broadcast here every Wednesday from 5-6PM. To help keep you updated we also have weekly Spotify playlists, including all the songs we play on the show plus a few more of our favorites.



TUNDRA RADIO

You can catch me on South Wales based Tundra Radio most weekends playing all sorts of stuff from 140-175 bpm. More details are on their Facebook and you can listen live on their website.



BLOC 2 BLOC RADIO

Livestreaming straight from my house every Thursday from 4-5PM on Bloc2Bloc Radio. This one is strictly DnB – Think old school breaks, techy rollers and summertime vibes.

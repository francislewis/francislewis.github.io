DJ

I play club focused, underground music in the general 140-175bpm range across a host of different genres from dubstep, grime and DnB to ghetto house, techno and every once in a while even a bit of donk. I don’t take myself too seriously, it’s all about having fun and making the crowd dance. It seems to be working well as I continue to support a host of different acts across several underground UK music scenes including; Kahn & Neek, Breakage, Ray Keith, DJ Steves, DJ Hybrid, Burt Cope, Born on Road & SP:MC. You can follow me on Facebook to see where I’m next playing live, listen to my radio residencies here or follow me on SoundCloud for my newest mixes.
